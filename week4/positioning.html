Acceptance

March 5, 2021

The greatest gift you can ever give yourself is your own love, your own gratitude and your own nuturing. We tend to make comparisons and feel like we do not "stack up" or maybe we feel like we do not belong and then we close ourselves from experinces. Many people shy away form yoga because of the imagery tied to it, the image social media gives it. It is essential to understand that is not YOGA, yoga truly means balancing the breath the breath with movement-ASANA! Open your heart to yourself and see how beautiful it is. Namaste!

Today is Perfect.

December 20, 2020

We all have different days, some days are looser than others. Do I even need to mention the tightness of others? I have had many students share their disappointment in the days they are feeling tight, tired, and frustrated. Elements vary throughout your days and weeks: How much water did you drink today? How much sleep did you get? Is it colder than usual outside? Are you having a rough week? So many variables change our bodies, there are no two days that are the same in every way. The most important thing you can do for yourself is be happy your there everyday, do not waste time wondering why and just give in to the joy of being here today, with yourself and your prana! Namaste!

Chant or Trap?

July 7 2020

The age old question: "What music is the most apprpriate for yoga?" Is it chanting? Om'ing? How about just good old silence and hear eachother breathe? In my years of teaching and attending practice sessions I can say that I think it all works! I teach a very high energy Vinyasa to uncensored hip hop, it's not for every body but the ones who enjoy it always say the same thing: "The music connects us all toegther like a party!" Most yoga practice sessions I have been to have been quiet, sometimes without music at all (I actually love that, it keeps your focus on your prana) I encourage you to find more classes with different vibes, you never know you may realize doing a sun salution to Drakes "Hotline Bling" may be one of the best things that has happened to your mat! Namaste!
